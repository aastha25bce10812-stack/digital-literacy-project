Do's and Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student.

Do's :
Think before posting - Ensure your content is appropriate and respectful
Protect your privacy – Use strong passwords and manage privacy settings.
Be respectful to others – Maintain polite and positive interactions.
Verify information – Check facts before sharing any news or content.
Use social media for learning – Follow educational and career-related pages.
Build a positive digital identity – Share achievements, projects, and skills.

Don'ts :
Don’t compare yourself constantly – Social media often shows unrealistic lifestyles.
Don’t overshare personal information – Avoid sharing sensitive details.
Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content – It can harm your reputation.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying – Avoid arguments, trolling, or harassment.
Don’t spread fake news – Always confirm sources before sharing.
Don’t rely on it for validation – Likes and comments don’t define your worth.
Don’t share others’ content without permission – Respect others’ privacy and rights.
